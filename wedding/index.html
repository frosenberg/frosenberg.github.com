How to get to the venue?

The ceremony venue and the reception venue are close to each other and only separated by a steep long hill :). However, both can easily be reached by car from the city of Vienna. Since parking is limited and there is plenty of drinking we suggest to leave the car at home.

To get to the venue you have two options:
  1. Take the subway U4 to "Heiligenstadt" and the take Bus 38A - Direction Kahlenberg where you get off at "Cobenzl". From there it is a 1 min walk to the vineyard.
  2. Take the Tram 38 to final stop Grinzing; from there you take Bus 38A - Direction "Kahlenberg" and get off at "Cobenzl". From there it is a 1 min walk to the vineyard.
After the ceremony, we have transportation organized to the reception venue (which is just down the hill in Grinzing).
